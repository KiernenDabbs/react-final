Information

Campaign

This is a custom campaign with many creatures to fight. A campaign is the journey your characters go on and can be anything you want it to be.

Creatures

Creatures are the enemies you face across your Dungeons and Dragons campaign. They can be an official monster from the DnD lore and campaigns, or they can be Homebrewed (see below) to suit any campaign you'd like.

Homebrew

Homebrewing is a type of content for DnD where you create everything. You can homebrew your character's class, you can homebrew enemies, or homebrew an entire campaign. It is entirely up to your preference how much you homebrew, but you want to follow closely to the statistics and guidelines of official DnD assets so your homebrew content does not become too imbalanced. Or you can completely ignore that and create a chaotic mess, so it's up to you! That's the fun part, experiment, get better, and have fun making your own game.



Below is a list of creatures for a Homebrewed campaign. Some are official creatures from the DnD database, and some are completely custom! You can select which ones to delete, which ones to keep, and you can upload your own custom creature. Choose your roster of enemies to fight!
